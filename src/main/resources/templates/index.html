Background

Our club, founded in 2017, has always been about seeking out young, capable students in Computer/Cyber Security majors that enjoy socializing about computers, code, hacks, linux, and other hacker-esque hobbies.

Goals

Our club has a strong emphasis on accomplishing the following tasks when we meet up as a group:

  • Scheduling future meetings
  • Planning events (Hackathon, Capture the Flag competitions, etc.)
  • Sharing ideas and findings regarding hacking discoveries
  • Sharing code and programs
  • Learning and teaching each other new languages
